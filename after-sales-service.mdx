## **4.1 Post-Trip Follow-Up**

**Timing:** Within 24 hours of ride completion

**Text Message Template:**

> Hi \[Customer Name\], thank you for riding with \[Company Name\]! We hope everything was perfect. If you have a moment, we'd love your feedback: \[Survey Link\]. Questions? Reply anytime.

**Email Template:**

```text
Subject: How Was Your Ride? – [Company Name]

Dear [Customer Name],

Thank you for choosing [Company Name] for your recent transportation. We hope [Driver Name] provided you with exceptional service.

We'd love to hear about your experience! Your feedback helps us maintain the highest standards:  
[SHARE YOUR FEEDBACK] (link to survey or review platform)

If there's anything we could have done better, please reply to this email—I read every response personally.

We look forward to serving you again soon!

Warm regards,  
[Your Name]
```

💡 **Info:** Prompt post-trip follow-up improves review rates and customer satisfaction.

## **4.3 Complaint Handling Process**

### **Step 1 – Acknowledge Immediately**

**Phone Script:**

> "Thank you for bringing this to my attention, \[Customer Name\]. I sincerely apologize that your experience didn't meet expectations. I want to make this right. Can you tell me more about what happened?"

**Email Template:**

```text
Subject: RE: [Original Subject] – We Want to Make This Right

Dear [Customer Name],

Thank you for sharing your feedback. I'm truly sorry to hear that your experience fell short of expectations. This is not the level of service we strive to provide, and I take your concerns seriously.

I'm looking into this matter personally and will follow up with you by [Time/Date] with a resolution.

In the meantime, please call me directly at [Phone Number] if you'd like to discuss further.

Sincerely,  
[Your Name]
```

### **Step 2 – Investigate**

| Task |
| :-- |
| Review trip records and driver notes |
| Check GPS/tracking data if applicable |
| Speak with driver |
| Review dispatch communications |

### **Step 3 – Resolve**

| Issue Type | Suggested Resolution |
| :-- | :-- |
| Driver late (15\+ min) | Partial refund (10–20%) or credit toward future ride |
| Driver behavior issue | Full investigation, formal warning/termination if warranted, 20–50% refund or full refund |
| Vehicle cleanliness | Apology \+ credit for next ride |
| Wrong vehicle sent | Partial refund \+ upgrade on next ride |
| Major service failure | Full refund \+ credit for future ride \+ personal call from manager |

### **Step 4 – Follow Up**

> "Hi \[Customer Name\], I wanted to follow up and confirm that \[resolution\] has been processed. Again, I sincerely apologize for your experience, and I hope you'll give us another opportunity to serve you in the future. If there's anything else I can do, please reach out."
