Baltimore/Washington International Airport (BWI) is a major airport serving both the Baltimore and Washington DC metropolitan areas. The airport has a simple single-terminal layout with connected concourses, making pickups easier and more straightforward compared to larger DMV airports.

## Pickup Instructions

1. Passenger proceeds to baggage claim after landing
2. Passenger exits to the lower-level ground transportation area
3. Passenger proceeds to the designated car service pickup area
4. Chauffeur meets passenger curbside or inside terminal for meet & greet reservations

---

## Important Notes

- Single-terminal layout makes navigation easier for passengers and chauffeurs
- All concourses are connected within the same terminal building
- Pickup occurs on the lower-level ground transportation curb
- Airport signage and terminal navigation are generally straightforward
- Traffic congestion is usually lighter compared to DCA and IAD
- Commonly used for both Baltimore and Washington DC travel needs
