title: "Booking & Service Questions"
---

### **Booking Questions**

| Customer Question | Suggested Staff Response |
| :-- | :-- |
| "How do I confirm the booking?" | "Once you're ready, we collect payment either by card over the phone or through a secure payment link sent by email. After payment, the reservation is confirmed." |
| "Can I reserve now and pay later?" | "We can note the request, but the booking is secured only once payment is received." |

---

### **Service Questions**

| Customer Question | Suggested Staff Response |
| :-- | :-- |
| "Will the driver contact me?" | "Yes, before pickup the driver or dispatch team will share the chauffeur details, and the driver may also contact you upon arrival if needed." |
| "What if my flight is delayed?" | "If flight details are provided, we monitor the flight and adjust the pickup accordingly." |
| "Can we add a stop?" | "Yes, in many cases we can add stops. Depending on the route and timing, there may be an additional charge." |
| "Can we drink in the vehicle?" | "That depends on the vehicle type and company policy. I can confirm that for you before booking." |
| "Do you provide child seats?" | "If child seats are available, we can note that request. I recommend advising us in advance so we can confirm properly." |
