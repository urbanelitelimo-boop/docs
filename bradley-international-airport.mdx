Bradley International Airport (BDL) is a mid-size regional airport serving the Hartford and Springfield areas. The airport has a simple layout with straightforward pickup procedures, making navigation easy for both passengers and chauffeurs.

## Pickup Instructions

1. Passenger proceeds to baggage claim after landing
2. Passenger exits to the ground transportation area outside the terminal
3. Chauffeur meets passenger at the designated curbside pickup area
4. Meet & greet passengers may be met inside the terminal arrivals area

---

## Important Notes

- Single-terminal layout makes navigation simple and easy
- Chauffeurs typically wait at the ground transportation curb outside baggage claim
- Traffic congestion is usually light compared to larger international airports
- Frequently used by Connecticut and western Massachusetts travelers
- Pickup coordination is generally quick and straightforward
