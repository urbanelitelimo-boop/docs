Chicago Midway International Airport (MDW) is a smaller and more straightforward airport compared to O'Hare International Airport. The airport operates through a single terminal, making navigation and limo pickups simple for both passengers and chauffeurs.

---

## Pickup Instructions

1. Passenger proceeds to baggage claim after landing
2. Passenger exits to the lower-level ground transportation area
3. Passenger proceeds to the designated car service pickup area
4. Chauffeur meets passenger curbside or inside terminal for meet & greet reservations

---

## Important Notes

- Single-terminal layout makes pickups quick and straightforward
- Southwest Airlines is the primary carrier operating at MDW
- Pickup occurs on the lower-level ground transportation curb outside baggage claim
- Airport navigation is significantly easier compared to O'Hare
- Traffic congestion and curbside delays are usually lighter than ORD
- Commonly preferred by Chicago travelers looking for a simpler airport experience
