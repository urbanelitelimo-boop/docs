# **1. Customer Handling & Tone Guidelines**

---

## **1.1 The Right Tone**

| Quality | Notes / Info |
| :-- | :-- |
| Warm | Make the customer feel welcomed and valued |
| Professional | Maintain a polished and courteous approach |
| Confident | Communicate knowledgeably and authoritatively |
| Fast | Respond promptly without hesitation |
| Helpful | Anticipate customer needs and offer solutions |
| Luxury-service oriented | Emphasize premium experience, not just transport |

💡 **Info:** A limo company sells **more than transportation** — you are offering:

- Reliability
- Comfort
- Professionalism
- Punctuality
- Safety
- Premium experience
- Peace of mind

**Pitch Example (Luxury-oriented):**

> "We make your transportation smooth, stress-free, and professionally managed from start to finish."

---

## **1.2 The 5-Step Customer Handling Formula**

| Step | Action | Script Example |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| 1 | Greet professionally | "Thank you for calling \[Company Name\], this is \[Agent Name\]. How may I assist you today?" |
| 2 | Understand trip requirement | "May I have your pickup date, time, and trip details?" |
| 3 | Build confidence & value | "Perfect, we do handle that type of service regularly." |
| 4 | Provide the quote clearly | "Your total for this trip would be \$\_\_\_. " |
| 5 | Ask for booking | "Would you like me to secure that booking for you now?" |
