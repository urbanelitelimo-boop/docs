## **3.1 Customer Email Asking for Quote**

**Perfect Reply Structure:**

- Thank them
- Confirm understanding
- Share quote
- Invite booking
- Offer help

**Perfect Pitch:**

```text
Dear [Customer Name],

Thank you for reaching out to [Company Name]. We would be pleased to assist with your transportation request. Based on the details provided, the quote for your trip is $___ for a [vehicle type].

If you would like to proceed, we can confirm the reservation for you right away. Please reply to this email or call us directly.

We look forward to assisting you.
```

---

## **3.2 Customer Asks if Vehicle is Available**

**Perfect Pitch:**

```text
Dear [Customer Name],

Thank you for your inquiry. Based on your requested date and time, we are currently [able / not able] to offer [vehicle type], subject to final confirmation at the time of booking.

If you'd like, I can help secure this for you right away.
```

---

## **3.3 Customer Asks for a Better Price by Email**

**Perfect Pitch:**

```text
Dear [Customer Name],

Thank you for your reply. I understand that pricing is an important factor. We always aim to provide the best balance of quality, reliability, and value.

I reviewed your request, and the best available rate I can offer is $___. Please let me know if you would like me to secure this option for you.
```

---

## **3.4 Customer Says They Need Time to Decide**

**Perfect Pitch:**

```text
Dear [Customer Name],

Absolutely, I understand. Please take your time reviewing the quote. If you have any questions or would like to discuss vehicle options, timing, or pricing further, I’d be happy to assist.

Since availability can change, I recommend confirming as soon as you're ready.
```

## **3.5 Customer Wants Payment Link**

**Perfect Pitch:**

```text
Dear [Customer Name],

Thank you for choosing [Company Name]. Please use the secure payment link below to complete your reservation:

[Payment Link]

Once payment is received, your booking will be confirmed and a confirmation email will be sent to you.
```

## **3.6 Customer Confirms Booking by Email**

**Perfect Reply:**

```text
Dear [Customer Name],

Thank you for your confirmation. We are pleased to proceed with your reservation.

Please find your booking details below:
[Trip Details]

To finalize the reservation, we can either process payment over the phone or send you a secure payment link by email.
```

## **3.7 Customer Asks Many Detailed Questions Before Booking**

**Common Questions:**

- Is gratuity included?
- Can the driver wait?
- Is food/drink allowed?
- Can we make extra stops?
- What happens if the flight is delayed?
- What is the cancellation policy?
- Is smoking allowed?
- What vehicle do you recommend?
- Where do we meet our driver?\

**Best Style for Answers:** Clear, organized, calm.

**Perfect Structure:**

```text
Dear [Customer Name],

Thank you for your questions. Please see below:

Gratuity: [answer]  
Waiting time: [answer]  
Extra stops: [answer]  
Flight delays: [answer]  
Cancellation policy: [answer]  

Please let me know once you'd like to proceed, and I’ll be happy to help finalize the reservation.
```

---

## **3.8 Customer Complains by Email**

**Perfect Pitch:**

```text
Dear [Customer Name],

Thank you for bringing this matter to our attention. I sincerely apologize that your experience did not meet expectations. We take concerns like this very seriously.

I am reviewing the matter and will follow up with you shortly with a proper response and resolution.
```
