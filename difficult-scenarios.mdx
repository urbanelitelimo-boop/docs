---
title: "Problematic Scenarios & Solutions"
---

## **1.1 Customer is Rude or Impatient**

**Pitch / Script:**

> "I understand your concern, and I want to help resolve this as quickly as possible."

💡 **Info:**

- Do **not argue**
- Stay calm, concise, and solution-focused

---

## **1.2 Customer Keeps Negotiating Heavily**

**Pitch / Script:**

> "I understand you're looking for the best possible rate. I’ve already reviewed the most competitive option available for this request, and I’d be happy to secure it for you if you'd like to proceed."

💡 **Info:**

- Acknowledge their concern without lowering perceived value
- Emphasize professionalism and reliability

---

## **1.3 Customer Goes Silent After Quote**

**Follow-Up Pitch:**

> "Just checking in regarding your transportation request. If you're still interested, I’d be happy to assist. If your plans changed, that’s completely fine as well."

💡 **Info:**

- Keep follow-ups **friendly and non-pressuring**
- Maintain **soft urgency** if needed

---

## **1.4 Customer Asks Why They Should Choose Your Company**

**Pitch / Script:**

> "Customers choose us because we focus on professionalism, punctuality, clean vehicles, and dependable service. We aim to make the entire experience easy and stress-free from booking to drop-off."

💡 **Info:**

- Highlight **unique value proposition**
- Reinforce **luxury and reliability**
