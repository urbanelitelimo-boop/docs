---
title: "Call Followup Script"
---

| Follow-Up Type | Suggested Staff Script |
| :-- | :-- |
| **5.1 First Follow-Up After Quote** | "Hi \[Customer Name\], this is \[Name\] from \[Company Name\]. I just wanted to follow up on the quote we sent for your upcoming trip and see if you had any questions." |
| **5.2 Follow-Up with Soft Pressure** | "I wanted to check in because availability can change, and I’d be happy to help secure the option for you if you're ready." |
| **5.3 Follow-Up with Discount** | "I reviewed your request and may be able to offer a better rate of \$\_\_\_ if you’d like to move forward today." |
| **5.4 Final Follow-Up** | "I just wanted to make one final follow-up regarding your transportation request. If you’re still interested, I’d be happy to help. If your plans changed, no problem at all." |
