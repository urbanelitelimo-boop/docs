## **2.1 Follow-Up Schedule for Pending Quotes**

| Day | Action | Channel |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Day 0 | Send initial quote | Email \+ Call |
| Day 1 | Follow-up call \+ Discount offer | Phone / Email |
| Day 2 | Follow-up email with discount offer | Email |
| Day 4 | Final follow-up call | Phone |
| Day 7 | "Last chance" email | Email |
| Day 14 | Final courtesy email | Email |

💡 **Info:** The schedule ensures timely follow-ups while avoiding lead fatigue.

---

## **Follow-Up Scenarios**

| Scenario | Instructions |
| :-- | :-- |
| **Same-Day / Next-Day Rides** | If the customer does not answer, send the best available discounted offer via SMS, Email, or other approved channels. |
| **Long-Term Bookings (1–3 Months in Advance)** | Proactively contact customers at least **three times per month**. In the last 10 days before booking, attempt 2–3 additional follow-ups, with a **2-day gap** between attempts. |
| **Engaged Customers** | Customers who have shown interest, requested info, negotiated pricing, or expressed intent are **high-priority leads**. Follow-up must adhere to promised date/time to maintain trust. Replies must be handled according to prior conversation. |
| **Follow-Up Priority Order** | 1. Engaged customers with scheduled follow-ups <br />2. Same-day / next-day rides <br />3. Customers traveling within 10 days <br />4. Rides scheduled 1–3 months in advance <br />5. General inquiries / cold leads |

## **2.2 Follow-Up Scripts & Templates**

**Day 1 — Phone Follow-Up:**

> "Hi \[Customer Name\], this is \[Your Name\] from \[Company Name\]. I wanted to follow up on the quote I sent yesterday for \[Date\]. Have you had a chance to review it?"

**If yes:**

> "Great! Do you have any questions I can answer? I'd love to help you get this booked."

**If no:**

> "No problem—I know things get busy. I can walk you through it now if you have a minute, or call back at a better time. What works for you?"

---

**Day 2 — Discounted Price Email:**

```text
Subject: A Special Offer for Your [Date] Transportation

Dear [Customer Name],

I wanted to follow up on your quote request for [Date]. As a thank-you for considering [Company Name], I'm pleased to offer you a [X]% discount if booked by [Deadline Date].

Your Updated Quote:
- Original Price: $[Amount]
- Your Price: $[Discounted Amount]
- Savings: $[Savings]

Reply to this email or call [Phone Number] to lock in this rate. Offer valid for 48 hours, subject to vehicle availability.

Warm regards,
[Your Name]
```

---

**Day 4 — Final Follow-Up Call:**

> "Hi \[Customer Name\], this is \[Your Name\] from \[Company Name\]. I'm reaching out one more time regarding your transportation for \[Date\]. I want to make sure we haven't missed the chance to serve you. Is this still something you're looking to book?"

**If customer declines:**

> "I completely understand. If anything changes or you need transportation in the future, please keep us in mind. We'd love to earn your business. Have a wonderful day!"

---

**Day 7 — "Last Chance" Email:**

```text
Subject: Availability Update for [Date]

Dear [Customer Name],

This is my final follow-up regarding your event on [Date]. Availability for [vehicle type] is limited, and I wanted to ensure you don’t miss out.

If you're still considering us, I’m happy to answer questions or process your reservation today.  
If you’ve decided otherwise, I wish you a wonderful event!

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

---

**Day 14 — Final Courtesy Email:**

```text
Subject: We're Here When You Need Us

Dear [Customer Name],

I hope you had a wonderful [event/trip]! This closes the loop on our previous conversations. Whenever you need premium ground transportation in the future, we'd be honored to serve you.

Feel free to save my contact information for your next occasion.

All the best,  
[Your Name]
```

## **2.3 CRM Tracking Requirements**

| Requirement | Details |
| :-- | :-- |
| **Date/Time of Contact** | Log each attempt |
| **Contact Method** | Call, Email, Text |
| **Outcome** | Connected, Voicemail, No Answer, Email Sent |
| **Customer Feedback / Objections** | Document any responses or objections |
| **Next Action Scheduled** | Specify date/time for next follow-up |
| **Status Update** | Hot, Warm, Cold, Lost, Converted |
