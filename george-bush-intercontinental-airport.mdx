title: "George Bush Intercontinental Airport"
---

George Bush Intercontinental Airport (IAH) is one of the primary international airports serving Houston, Texas. The airport has multiple terminals connected internally, with separate pickup areas for limo and black car services at each terminal.

## Pickup Instructions

1. Passenger proceeds to baggage claim after landing
2. Passenger exits to the arrivals/lower level
3. Passenger proceeds to the designated ground transportation pickup area
4. Chauffeur meets passenger curbside or inside terminal for meet & greet reservations

---

## Important Notes

- Always confirm the passenger’s exact terminal before dispatching the chauffeur
- United Airlines primarily operates from Terminals B, C, and E
- Terminal D mainly handles international arrivals and customs processing
- Pickup occurs on the lower arrivals level outside each terminal
- Underground Subway train connects terminals internally
- Traffic congestion and terminal delays are common during peak travel periods
