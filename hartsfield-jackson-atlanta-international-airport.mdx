---
title: "Hartsfield–Jackson Atlanta International Airport"
---

Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport (ATL) is the busiest airport in the world by passenger traffic and one of the largest transportation hubs in the United States. Due to heavy passenger volume and strict curbside enforcement, pickups at ATL require fast coordination and clear passenger communication.

---

| Item | Details | Explanation |

---

## Pickup Instructions

1. Passenger proceeds to baggage claim after landing
2. Passenger exits to the lower-level ground transportation area
3. Passenger proceeds to the designated car service pickup area
4. Chauffeur meets passenger curbside or near the designated pickup zone
5. International passengers arriving through Concourse F follow the same lower-level pickup process

---

## Important Notes

- ATL is the busiest airport in the world and can become extremely crowded
- Customers should be ready for pickup immediately after exiting the terminal
- Chauffeurs cannot wait curbside for long periods due to strict airport enforcement
- International arrivals use Concourse F, which is separate from the domestic terminal
- Ground transportation pickup occurs on the lower level
- Heavy congestion and fast-moving curb traffic are common throughout the day
- Clear communication between chauffeur and passenger is very important at ATL
