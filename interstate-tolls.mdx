# New York / Westchester → Connecticut (CT)

## Common Route Options

| Route | Main Highways | Estimated Tolls | Best For | Important Notes |
| :-- | :-- | :-- | :-- | :-- |
| I-95 North | I-95 N / CT Turnpike | ~\$6–\$8 | Fastest & most direct route | Heavy traffic near NYC and Stamford common |
| Merritt Parkway | CT Route 15 | No tolls | Lower-cost & scenic route | Usually 30–45 mins slower |
| Inland Route | I-684 → I-84 | Minimal tolls | Avoiding coastal traffic | Longer but smoother during peak congestion |

---

## Operational Notes

| Situation | Recommendation |
| :-- | :-- |
| Rush hour traffic near Stamford | Consider Merritt Parkway or Inland Route |
| Customer prioritizes speed | Use I-95 |
| Customer wants lower toll cost | Use Merritt Parkway |
| Heavy coastal congestion | Use I-684 / I-84 inland option |

# NYC / Long Island → New Jersey (NJ)

## Common Route Options

| Route | Main Crossing | Estimated Tolls | Best For | Important Notes |
| :-- | :-- | :-- | :-- | :-- |
| George Washington Bridge | GWB → NJ Turnpike | ~\$13–\$17 | Fastest North NJ access | Very heavy rush-hour congestion |
| Lincoln Tunnel | Midtown Manhattan → NJ | ~\$15–\$17 | Midtown NYC departures | Toll charged entering NYC only |
| Holland Tunnel | Downtown Manhattan → NJ | ~\$15–\$17 | Lower Manhattan access | Similar congestion to Lincoln Tunnel |
| NJ Turnpike | I-95 / NJTP | Distance-based | Long-distance NJ travel | Toll increases with travel distance |

---

## Operational Notes

| Situation | Recommendation |
| :-- | :-- |
| Midtown pickup/drop-off | Lincoln Tunnel usually preferred |
| North Jersey destinations | GWB often fastest |
| Heavy Manhattan congestion | Check tunnel wait times before dispatch |
| Long-distance NJ trips | Expect higher NJ Turnpike tolls |

# Interstate Route Planning Rules

| Rule | Explanation |
| :-- | :-- |
| Never assume shortest route is fastest | Traffic conditions may change route efficiency significantly |
| Always check real-time traffic before dispatch | NYC, NJ, and Boston corridors change rapidly during rush hours |
| Toll costs should be considered during quoting | Interstate pricing may vary significantly by route |
| Large airports increase route complexity | JFK, LAX, ORD, and DFW may add major delays |
| E-ZPass reduces toll delays and costs | Toll-by-plate is usually more expensive |
| Customer priorities matter | Some customers prefer faster routes, others prefer lower cost |
