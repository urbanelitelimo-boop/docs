---
title: "John F. Kennedy International Airport (JFK)"
---

JFK is one of the busiest international airports in the United States and a major airport for New York City operations. The airport contains multiple terminals with separate pickup zones for limo and black car services.

---

| Item | Details | Explanation |

| Airport Code | JFK | Standard airport code used for bookings, dispatch, and flight tracking. |

| Meet & Greet Service | Available | Chauffeur may wait inside the terminal with a name sign for meet & greet reservations. |
| Traffic Conditions | Heavy congestion common | Pickup and terminal access delays are common during peak travel hours. |

---

## Pickup Instructions

1. Passenger proceeds to baggage claim after landing
2. After collecting luggage, passenger goes to the arrivals/lower level
3. Passenger proceeds to the designated car service pickup area
4. Chauffeur meets passenger at the pickup zone or inside terminal for meet & greet bookings

---

## Important Notes

- Always confirm the exact terminal before dispatching the chauffeur
- Customers should remain at their arrival terminal for pickup
- Car service pickup areas are separate from taxi and rideshare pickup areas
- International arrivals may require additional customs clearance time
- Heavy congestion is common during peak hours
