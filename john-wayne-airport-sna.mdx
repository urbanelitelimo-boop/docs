John Wayne Airport (SNA) is a mid-size airport serving Orange County and the Southern California market. The airport has a simple single-terminal layout with relatively light congestion, making pickups easier and more efficient compared to larger airports like LAX.

## Pickup Instructions

1. Passenger proceeds to baggage claim after landing
2. Passenger exits to the lower arrivals level
3. Passenger proceeds to the ground transportation curb
4. Chauffeur meets passenger curbside or near the baggage claim exit for meet & greet reservations

---

## Important Notes

- Single-terminal layout makes airport navigation simple and efficient
- Pickup occurs on the lower arrivals level outside baggage claim
- Car service pickups operate from the ground transportation curb
- Traffic congestion is significantly lighter compared to LAX
- Chauffeurs commonly wait curbside or just outside the baggage claim exit
- Frequently preferred for Orange County and South Los Angeles travel due to easier airport access
