---
title: "LaGuardia Airport (LGA)"
---



LaGuardia Airport (LGA) is one of the primary airports serving New York City and has recently undergone major modernization and terminal upgrades. The airport contains multiple terminals with designated curbside pickup areas for limo and black car services.

---

## Airport Information

| Item | Details | Explanation |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Airport Full Name | LaGuardia Airport | Major domestic airport serving New York City. |
| Airport Code | LGA | Standard airport code used for reservations, dispatch, and flight tracking. |
| Airport Type | Domestic / Regional International Airport | Primarily handles domestic flights with limited international operations. |
| Location | Queens, New York | Located closer to Manhattan compared to JFK, making it popular for business travel. |
| Main Terminal | Terminal B | Most major airlines operate from Terminal B. |
| Delta Airlines Terminals | Terminals C & D | Delta primarily operates from Terminals C and D, which are internally connected to Terminal B. |
| Passenger Pickup Location | Arrivals Level | Passengers are picked up outside baggage claim on the arrivals level curb. |
| Car Service Pickup Area | Designated curbside pickup zones | Limo and black car pickups occur at designated pickup areas outside each terminal. |
| Terminal Connection | Connected through covered walkways | Certain terminals are internally connected, allowing passenger movement between terminals. |
| Traffic Conditions | Moderate to heavy congestion | Traffic congestion is common during peak travel hours and airport construction periods. |

---

## Pickup Instructions

1. Passenger proceeds to baggage claim after landing
2. After collecting luggage, passenger exits to the arrivals level curb
3. Passenger proceeds to the designated car service pickup area
4. Chauffeur meets passenger curbside or inside terminal for meet & greet reservations

---

## Important Notes

- Always verify the passenger’s terminal before dispatching the chauffeur
- Terminal layouts at LGA have changed significantly after modernization
- Terminal B is the primary hub for most airlines
- Delta passengers commonly arrive at Terminals C or D
- Pickup occurs on the arrivals level outside the terminal
- Traffic congestion and curbside delays are common during busy hours
