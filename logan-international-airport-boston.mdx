---
title: "Logan International Airport (Boston)"
---

Logan International Airport (BOS) is the primary airport serving Boston, Massachusetts and one of the busiest airports in the New England region. The airport has multiple terminals with separate arrivals areas and designated pickup zones for limo and black car services.

---

---

## Pickup Instructions

1. Passenger proceeds to baggage claim after landing
2. Passenger exits to the arrivals/lower level curb
3. Passenger proceeds to the designated car service pickup area
4. Chauffeur meets passenger curbside or inside terminal for meet & greet reservations

---

## Important Notes

- Always confirm the passenger’s exact terminal before dispatching the chauffeur
- Terminal E is separate from domestic terminals and primarily handles international arrivals
- Each terminal has its own dedicated pickup curb
- Car service pickup areas are separate from taxi and rideshare pickup zones
- Silver Line shuttle service connects airport terminals internally
- Customers should remain at their arrival terminal for pickup
- Traffic congestion and delays are common during busy travel periods
