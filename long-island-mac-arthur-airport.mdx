Long Island MacArthur Airport (ISP) is a small regional airport serving Long Island, New York. The airport has a simple single-terminal layout with compact arrivals and baggage claim areas, making pickups quick and easy for both passengers and chauffeurs.

## Pickup Instructions

1. Passenger proceeds to baggage claim after landing
2. Passenger exits directly through the arrivals area
3. Chauffeur meets passenger curbside outside the terminal exit
4. Pickup process is usually fast with minimal congestion and walking distance

## Important Notes

- Single-terminal layout makes passenger navigation extremely easy
- Chauffeurs typically wait directly outside the arrivals exit

- Low congestion allows for quicker pickups compared to larger NYC airports
