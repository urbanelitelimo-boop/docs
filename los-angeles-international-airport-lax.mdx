---
title: "Los Angeles International Airport (LAX)"
---

Los Angeles International Airport (LAX) is one of the busiest and most complex airports in the United States. Unlike most airports, limo and black car pickups are not allowed directly at the terminal curb. All car service pickups operate through a separate pickup facility called LAX-it.

## Pickup Instructions

1. Passenger lands and collects baggage
2. Passenger exits the terminal and follows signs for LAX-it
3. Passenger takes the free LAX-it shuttle bus
4. Chauffeur waits at the designated car service pickup area inside LAX-it
5. Passenger meets chauffeur at the assigned pickup zone

---

## Important Notes

- Direct terminal curbside pickup is not allowed for limo and black car services
- All car service pickups operate through the LAX-it facility
- Customers must use the free shuttle bus to reach the pickup area
- Always inform passengers about the LAX-it process before arrival
- Allow an additional 10–15 minutes for shuttle transportation and pickup coordination
- Tom Bradley International Terminal (TBIT) passengers follow the same pickup procedure
- Heavy congestion and delays are common throughout the airport area
