---
title: "Miami International Airport (MIA)"
---

Miami International Airport (MIA) is one of the busiest international airports in the United States and a major gateway to Latin America and the Caribbean. The airport has a large terminal layout with heavy international traffic and designated pickup areas for limo and black car services.

## Pickup Instructions

1. Passenger proceeds to baggage claim after landing
2. International passengers complete customs and immigration processing if required
3. Passenger exits to the lower-level ground transportation area
4. Passenger proceeds to the designated car service pickup area
5. Chauffeur meets passenger curbside or inside terminal for meet & greet reservations

---

## Important Notes

- Airport operates through one main terminal divided into three concourse areas
- Heavy international traffic can create longer customs and baggage claim wait times
- Pickup occurs on the lower-level ground transportation curb
- Skytrain connects concourses internally within the airport
- MIA Mover connects the airport to nearby transportation facilities
- Traffic congestion and pickup delays are common during busy travel hours
