Newark Liberty International Airport (EWR) is one of the primary airports serving the New York and New Jersey area. The airport has multiple terminals with separate roadway access and designated pickup areas for limo and black car services.

---

---

## Pickup Instructions

1. Passenger proceeds to baggage claim after landing
2. After collecting luggage, passenger goes to the arrivals/lower level
3. Passenger proceeds to the designated car service pickup area
4. Chauffeur meets passenger curbside or inside terminal for meet & greet reservations

---

## Important Notes

- Always confirm the passenger’s exact terminal before dispatching the chauffeur
- Terminals A, B, and C are completely separate by road access
- Car service pickup areas are different from taxi and rideshare pickup zones
- AirTrain connects terminals internally but passengers should remain at their arrival terminal
- Heavy congestion and curbside delays are common during peak hours
