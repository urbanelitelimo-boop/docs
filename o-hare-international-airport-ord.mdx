title: "O'Hare International Airport (ORD)"
---

O'Hare International Airport (ORD) is one of the busiest airports in the United States and a major international hub serving Chicago and the Midwest region. The airport has separate domestic and international terminals with designated pickup areas for limo and black car services.

## Pickup Instructions

1. Passenger proceeds to baggage claim after landing
2. International passengers complete customs and immigration at Terminal 5 if required
3. Passenger exits to the arrivals/lower level
4. Passenger proceeds to the designated car service pickup area
5. Chauffeur meets passenger curbside or inside terminal for meet & greet reservations

---

## Important Notes

- Always confirm the passenger’s exact terminal before dispatching the chauffeur
- Terminal 5 is completely separate from domestic terminals 1, 2, and 3
- Domestic terminals are connected through underground walkways
- ATS train system connects all airport terminals internally
- Customers should remain at their arrival terminal for pickup
- Pickup occurs on the lower-level designated car service curb
- Heavy congestion and curbside delays are common during peak travel periods
