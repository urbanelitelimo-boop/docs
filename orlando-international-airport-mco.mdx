---
title: "Orlando International Airport (MCO)"
---

Orlando International Airport (MCO) is one of the busiest airports in Florida and a major destination airport for tourism and family travel. The airport has a unique layout where passengers travel from airside terminals to the main terminal before reaching baggage claim and ground transportation areas.

---

---

## Pickup Instructions

1. Passenger lands at their assigned airside terminal
2. Passenger takes the airport shuttle/train to the main terminal
3. Passenger proceeds to baggage claim in the main terminal
4. Passenger exits to the lower-level ground transportation area
5. Chauffeur meets passenger at the designated car service pickup area

---

## Important Notes

- All passengers must return to the main terminal before exiting the airport
- Shuttle transportation from airsides to the main terminal is part of the normal arrival process
- Terminal C operates separately and is used by select airlines
- Always confirm the passenger’s exact terminal before dispatching the chauffeur
- Pickup occurs on the lower-level ground transportation curb
- Traffic congestion increases heavily during holidays and peak tourism seasons
