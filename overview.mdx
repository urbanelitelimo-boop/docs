The Knowledge Base covers all major areas of the business, including fleet and vehicle categories, passenger and luggage capacities, premium transportation services, airport operations, pricing structures, dispatch procedures, customer service standards, booking workflows, flight tracking, driver coordination, and operational policies. It also includes information related to locations, airports, hotels, tourist destinations, tolls, special requests, and luxury service features such as chauffeur services, child seats, multilingual drivers, wheelchair accessibility, luxury SUVs, limousines, Sprinter vans, and group transportation options. In addition, the KB provides guidance regarding company policies, compliance procedures, cancellations, refunds, emergency handling, and communication standards to ensure employees are fully prepared to deliver a smooth, professional, and high-quality customer experience.

### Key Areas Covered in the Knowledge Base

- Fleet categories and vehicle knowledge
- Passenger and luggage capacity understanding
- Airport transfers and flight tracking procedures
- Booking, quotation, and dispatch workflow
- Customer service and communication standards
- Pricing structure and special request handling
- Luxury features and premium transportation services
- Driver coordination and operational procedures
- Policies, compliance, and emergency management
- Location, airport, hotel, and destination knowledge

## **1.4 Service Mindset**

| Behavior | Details / Examples |
| :-- | :-- |
| Never Sound Irritated | Remain calm and polite under pressure |
| Never Rush the Customer | Allow time for questions and decision-making |
