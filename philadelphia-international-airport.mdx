title: "Philadelphia International Airport"
---

Philadelphia International Airport (PHL) is the primary airport serving Philadelphia and surrounding Pennsylvania and New Jersey areas. The airport has multiple terminals connected internally, with separate pickup areas for limo and black car services at each terminal.

## Pickup Instructions

1. Passenger proceeds to baggage claim after landing
2. Passenger exits to the arrivals/lower level
3. Passenger proceeds to the designated ground transportation pickup area
4. Chauffeur meets passenger curbside or inside terminal for meet & greet reservations

---

## Important Notes

- Always confirm the passenger’s exact terminal before dispatching the chauffeur
- Terminal A handles international arrivals and customs processing
- Terminals B through F primarily handle domestic airline operations
- All terminals are connected internally after security screening
- Pickup occurs on the lower-level ground transportation curb outside each terminal
- Traffic congestion and curbside delays may occur during busy travel periods
