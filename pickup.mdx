

Airport pickup is when the chauffeur collects the passenger from the airport after arrival and transports them to their destination. Proper communication, terminal confirmation, and pickup coordination are critical for smooth airport operations.

---

# Pre-Arrival Rules

| Rule | Standard Procedure | Why It Matters |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Terminal Confirmation | Always confirm the passenger’s arrival terminal or collect the flight number during reservation. | Airports may have multiple terminals with separate pickup locations. |
| Flight Number Collection | Flight information must be collected for all airport pickups. | Allows dispatch and chauffeur to monitor flight status live. |
| Reminder Communication | Send reminder messages 24 hours and 2 hours before pickup. | Helps reduce passenger confusion and improves coordination. |
| Chauffeur Information | Provide chauffeur name, vehicle model, and license plate before pickup. | Helps passengers identify the correct vehicle quickly. |
| Flight Tracking | Chauffeur or dispatch should track the flight live. | Allows adjustment for delays, early arrivals, or terminal changes. |
| Pickup Type Confirmation | Confirm whether the customer wants curbside pickup or meet & greet service. | Pickup procedures and pricing may differ depending on service type. |

---

# Types of Airport Pickups

| Pickup Type | Description | Best For |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Curbside Pickup | Passenger exits terminal independently and meets chauffeur outside at the pickup curb. | Domestic travelers and standard airport pickups. |
| Inside Pickup / Meet & Greet | Chauffeur parks, enters terminal, greets passenger inside arrivals area, and escorts them to the vehicle. | VIP, international, elderly, family, and executive travelers. |

---

# 1. Curbside Pickup Rules

## How Curbside Pickup Works

| Step | Procedure | Important Notes |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Flight Monitoring | Chauffeur or dispatch tracks flight status live. | Monitor delays, early arrivals, gate changes, and terminal updates. |
| Chauffeur Waiting Area | Chauffeur waits at staging lot, cell phone lot, or holding area. | Vehicles should not wait at the terminal curb for long periods. |
| Passenger Ready Notification | Passenger contacts chauffeur after collecting luggage and exiting terminal. | Communication may happen through call, text, dispatch, or automated SMS. |
| Passenger Pickup | Chauffeur confirms passenger identity, assists with luggage, and departs promptly. | Airport curbs are active traffic zones with strict enforcement. |

---

## Advantages of Curbside Pickup

| Advantage | Explanation |
| :-- | :-- |
| Faster Turnaround | Pickups are usually completed quickly. |
| Lower Cost | Usually avoids parking and meet & greet charges. |
| Efficient During Light Traffic | Works well at smaller or less congested airports. |

---

## Common Challenges

| Challenge | Explanation |
| :-- | :-- |
| Passenger Confusion | Passengers may exit the wrong terminal or stand at the wrong curb. |
| Airport Congestion | Heavy traffic may delay curb access and pickups. |
| Communication Problems | Passengers without roaming, battery, or app access may be difficult to contact. |

---

# 2. Inside Pickup / Meet & Greet Rules

Inside pickup, also called Meet & Greet service, is a premium airport pickup where the chauffeur enters the terminal and escorts the passenger to the vehicle.

---

## How Meet & Greet Works

| Step | Procedure | Important Notes |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Vehicle Parking | Chauffeur parks in authorized airport parking. | Illegal curb waiting is not allowed. |
| Terminal Entry | Chauffeur enters terminal with name sign or branded signage. | Professional presentation is required. |
| Passenger Greeting | Chauffeur meets passenger at baggage claim, customs exit, or arrivals hall. | Meeting point depends on airport layout. |
| Luggage Assistance | Chauffeur assists passenger with baggage and luggage cart if needed. | Full-service assistance is expected. |
| Escort to Vehicle | Chauffeur escorts passenger to parked vehicle or pickup zone. | Creates a premium hospitality experience. |

---

# Meet & Greet Operational Rules

| Rule | Standard Procedure | Why It Matters |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Early Arrival Requirement | Chauffeur should arrive 15–30 minutes before flight arrival. | Allows time for parking and terminal access. |
| Professional Appearance | Chauffeur should maintain formal attire and polished presentation. | Meet & greet service represents luxury hospitality. |
| Correct Signage | Client name sign should be clean, visible, and professionally formatted. | Helps passengers identify chauffeur quickly. |
| Parking Compliance | Chauffeur must use authorized parking areas only. | Airports actively enforce commercial vehicle rules. |

---

# Best Situations for Meet & Greet Service

| Recommended For | Reason |
| :-- | :-- |
| International Travelers | Helpful for customs delays and communication issues. |
| VIP & Executive Clients | Provides premium hospitality experience. |
| Elderly Passengers | Offers additional assistance and easier navigation. |
| Families with Children | Reduces airport stress and luggage handling difficulties. |
| First-Time Visitors | Helps passengers unfamiliar with the airport or city. |
| Large Luggage Volume | Provides better baggage handling assistance. |

---

# Pickup Type Comparison

| Feature | Curbside Pickup | Meet & Greet Pickup |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Chauffeur Enters Terminal | No | Yes |
| Parking Required | Usually No | Yes |
| Passenger Assistance | Basic | Full Service |
| Pickup Speed | Faster | Slower |
| Cost | Lower | Premium |
| Best For | Domestic Travelers | VIP & International Travelers |
| Luggage Assistance | Basic Help | Full Assistance |
| Airport Navigation Help | Limited | Extensive |

---

# Important Operational Notes

- Never assume all airports operate the same way
- Every airport has different:
  - pickup zones
  - terminal layouts
  - enforcement policies
  - commercial vehicle rules
- Large airports may require additional coordination and communication
- Clear passenger communication is one of the most important parts of airport pickups
- Flight tracking should always be active for airport reservations
- Chauffeurs should avoid extended curb waiting unless airport policy allows it
