## **2.1 New Customer Calling for a Quote**

**Customer Situation:** First-time caller requesting pricing.\
**Staff Objectives:**

- Collect details
- Build trust
- Quote accurately
- Move toward booking

**Perfect Pitch:**

> "Thank you for calling \[Company Name\], this is \[Name\]. I'd be happy to help you with your transportation. May I get a few details so I can provide you with the best quote?"

**Questions to Ask:**

| Question |
| :-- |
| What date is the trip? |
| What is the pickup time? |
| What is the pickup location? |
| What is the drop-off location? |
| Is it one-way or round trip? |
| How many passengers will be traveling? |
| How much luggage do you have? |
| What type of vehicle are you looking for? |
| Is this for airport transfer, wedding, prom, corporate, cruise, or special event? |
| Do you have any special requests? |

**Quote Presentation Pitch:**

> "Based on the details you provided, the total for this trip would be \$\_\_\_ in a \[vehicle type\]. This includes professional chauffeur service and a smooth, reliable ride for your schedule."

**Closing Pitch:**

> "Would you like me to go ahead and secure this for you now?"

## **2.2 Website Lead Submitted Form – Call Customer**

**Customer Situation:** Submitted form but did not call.\
**Staff Objectives:**

- Contact quickly
- Confirm details
- Build confidence
- Convert lead into booking

**Perfect Pitch:**

> "Hi \[Customer Name\], this is \[Agent Name\] from \[Company Name\]. Thank you for reaching out through our website for your transportation request. I’m calling to go over the details and help you with the best option for your trip."

**If They Are Busy:**

> "No problem at all. I can quickly confirm the details and email everything over to you, or I can call you back at a better time. What works best for you?"

**If They Want the Quote Immediately:**

> "Absolutely. Based on your request, your quote is \$\_\_\_ for a \[vehicle type\]. If you'd like, I can also send it by email so you have everything in writing."

---

## **2.3 Customer Asks Only for Cheapest Price**

**Customer Situation:** Price-focused.\
**Staff Objectives:**

- Answer without sounding defensive
- Shift focus from price to value
- Keep them engaged

**Perfect Pitch:**

> "I understand price is important. We always try to provide a competitive rate while also making sure you receive professional, reliable, and on-time service. Let me get your trip details first so I can give you the best available option."

**If They Compare with Competitors:**

> "Of course, and that makes sense. When comparing rates, I always recommend checking whether gratuity, waiting time, and other fees are included, because some companies quote low upfront and add charges later. We prefer to be clear from the start."

---

## **2.4 Customer Says “I am Checking Around”**

**Customer Situation:** Shopping multiple companies.\
**Staff Objectives:**

- Avoid pressure
- Keep control
- Create trust
- Leave a strong impression

**Perfect Pitch:**

> "That’s completely understandable. Most customers do compare options. What we focus on is making sure the service is professional, the vehicles are well maintained, and the trip is handled smoothly from booking to drop-off. I’d be happy to send you the full quote in writing so you can compare everything clearly."

**Soft Close:**

> "If you'd like, I can also note your quote and keep this available for you while you decide."

---

## **2.5 Customer Says “Your Price is Too High”**

**Customer Situation:** Objection to pricing.\
**Staff Objectives:**

- Hold value
- Explore budget
- Offer alternatives if possible
- Save the lead

**Perfect Pitch:**

> "I understand, and I appreciate your honesty. Our pricing reflects professional chauffeur service, reliable scheduling, and a premium transportation experience. That said, let me see whether there is another vehicle option or a better package that can work for your budget."

**Stronger Rescue Pitch:**

> "If the current option feels a little high, I may be able to suggest a different vehicle or adjusted service plan that still gives you dependable transportation at a better rate."

**If Discount is Approved:**

> "I was able to review it for you, and the best I can offer is \$\_\_\_. I’d be happy to secure that for you today."

---

## **2.6 Customer Says “I Need to Ask My Spouse / Boss / Group”**

**Customer Situation:** Interested, but decision is not final.\
**Staff Objectives:**

- Keep momentum
- Set follow-up
- Avoid losing the lead

**Perfect Pitch:**

> "Absolutely, I understand. Since this is an important booking, it makes sense to confirm with everyone involved. I can send you the full quote right away, and I can also follow up with you tomorrow after you've had a chance to discuss it."

**Best Follow-Up Line:**

> "What would be the best time for me to check back with you?"

---

## **2.7 Customer Says “Send Me the Quote by Email”**

**Customer Situation:** Interested but not ready to close on call.\
**Staff Objectives:**

- Get email
- Set expectation
- Create follow-up point

**Perfect Pitch:**

> "Absolutely, I can do that for you. I’ll send the full quote with all trip details shortly, so you have everything in writing. After you review it, I’ll follow up in case you have any questions."

**Add Urgency Politely:**

> "Availability can change quickly depending on the date, so if you’d like me to hold the option for you, just let me know."

---

## **2.8 Customer Wants to Book Immediately**

**Customer Situation:** Hot lead, ready to close.\
**Staff Objectives:**

- Move quickly
- Collect full info
- Take payment
- Send confirmation

**Perfect Pitch:**

> "Perfect, I’d be happy to secure that for you now. Let me collect the booking details and finalize everything properly for you."

**Booking Questions:**

- Passenger full name
- Contact phone number
- Email address
- Pickup address
- Drop-off address
- Date and time
- Flight info (if airport)
- Passenger count
- Luggage count (if airport)
- Special instructions

**Payment Pitch:**

> "To confirm the reservation, we can either process the card over the phone now, or I can send you a secure payment link by email if you prefer to complete it yourself."

---

## **2.9 Customer Wants to Pay by Card Over the Phone**

**Perfect Pitch:**

> "Certainly. I’ll process the payment securely now and once it’s approved, your reservation will be fully confirmed and a confirmation email will be sent to you."

**Important Handling Line:**

> "Before I process the payment, let me quickly confirm the booking details with you one more time."

---

## **2.10 Customer Wants Payment Link Instead**

**Perfect Pitch:**

> "No problem at all. I’ll send you a secure payment link to your email right away. Once the payment is completed, your reservation will be confirmed."

**Follow-Up Line:**

> "As soon as you complete it, we’ll finalize the booking in our system and send your confirmation."

---

## **2.11 Customer Asks “Can You Hold the Vehicle Without Payment?”**

**Customer Situation:** Interested but not ready to commit.\
**Staff Objectives:**

- Avoid false reservation
- Encourage deposit/payment
- Stay polite

**Perfect Pitch:**

> "We’d be happy to note your request and keep the quote active, but the booking becomes fully secured only once payment is received. Since availability can change, I would recommend confirming as soon as you're ready."

**If Allow Short Hold:**

> "I can place it on a temporary courtesy hold for \[time period\], subject to availability, but final confirmation does require payment."

---

## **2.12 Customer Asks About Cancellation Policy**

**Perfect Pitch:**

> "Our cancellation policy is if you cancel within 24 hours we’ll fully refund you. We always recommend reviewing it before confirming, and I’ll also include it in writing with your booking confirmation so everything is clear."

**Best Practice:** Always sound **transparent, not defensive**.

---

## **2.13 Customer Asks If Gratuity / Tolls / Fees Are Included**

**Perfect Pitch:**

> "Let me clarify that for you. The quoted amount includes \[gratuity / tolls\]. We always like to be upfront so there are no surprises."

💡 **Info:** Hidden fee confusion kills trust — always clarify in limo sales.

---

## **2.14 Customer Asks About Vehicle Type and Capacity**

**Perfect Pitch:**

> "For \[number\] passengers, I would recommend a \[vehicle type\]. It gives you the right balance of comfort, space, and overall experience for that trip."

**Upsell Version:**

> "If you'd like a more premium experience, we also have \[higher vehicle category\], which is a great option for this type of occasion."

---

## **2.15 Customer Asks for Last-Minute Booking**

**Customer Situation:** Same-day or urgent booking.\
**Staff Objectives:**

- Act fast
- Check operationally
- Build urgency
- Close quickly

**Perfect Pitch:**

> "We do handle last-minute reservations depending on availability. Let me get your trip details quickly and check the best available option for you."

**If Available:**

> "We do have an option available, and I’d recommend securing it as soon as possible so we can lock it in."

**If Not Available:**

> "At the moment, that exact option is not available, but I may be able to offer you an alternative vehicle or adjusted timing."

---

## **2.16 Customer Asks for Airport Pickup**

**Perfect Pitch:**

> "Yes, we handle airport transfers regularly. We’ll just need your flight number, arrival time, and terminal details so we can schedule everything properly and monitor your flight if needed."

**Confidence Line:**

> "Airport service is one of the most important areas where reliability matters, and we make sure the trip is handled professionally."

---

## **2.17 Customer Asks for Wedding / Prom / Event Service**

**Perfect Pitch:**

> "Absolutely, we provide transportation for weddings, proms, birthdays, corporate events, and other special occasions. We’d love to help make the experience smooth, elegant, and stress-free."

**Event Trust-Builder:**

> "For special events, we pay close attention to timing, presentation, and coordination so everything runs properly on the day."

---

## **2.18 Customer Is Upset About a Previous Experience**

**Customer Situation:** Complaint or frustration.\
**Staff Objectives:**

- Calm them
- Listen
- Apologize
- Gather facts
- Move toward resolution

**Perfect Pitch:**

> "I’m very sorry to hear that, and I appreciate you bringing it to our attention. Please allow me to understand exactly what happened so I can help address it properly."

**While Listening:**

> "I understand."\
> "Thank you for explaining that."\
> "I can see why that would be frustrating."

**Resolution Line:**

> "Let me review the details and speak with the appropriate team member if needed. We take service concerns seriously, and we want to resolve this properly for you."

---

## **2.19 Customer Says Driver Is Late**

**Perfect Pitch:**

> "I sincerely apologize for the inconvenience. Let me check the live status of the trip and update you right away."

**Follow-Up After Checking:**

> "Thank you for your patience. The driver is approximately \[X\] minutes away. I understand the urgency and apologize again for the delay."

**If Serious Delay:**

> "We understand this has affected your plans, and we will make sure this is properly documented and reviewed."

---

## **2.20 Customer Wants to Change Trip Details After Booking**

**Perfect Pitch:**

> "Certainly, let me review your reservation details and check what can be updated for you. Depending on the change, there may be an adjustment in availability or pricing, and I’ll explain everything clearly before confirming any update."
