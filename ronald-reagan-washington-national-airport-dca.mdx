title: "Ronald Reagan Washington National Airport (DCA)"
---

Ronald Reagan Washington National Airport (DCA) is one of the primary airports serving the Washington DC metropolitan area. The airport is smaller and easier to navigate compared to larger international airports, making it a preferred airport for many business and government travelers.

## Pickup Instructions

1. Passenger proceeds to baggage claim after landing
2. Passenger exits to the arrivals/lower level
3. Passenger proceeds to the designated ground transportation pickup area
4. Chauffeur meets passenger curbside or inside terminal for meet & greet reservations

---

## Important Notes

- Terminals B and C are connected internally while Terminal A is separate
- Pickup occurs on the arrivals/lower level outside each terminal
- Ground transportation areas are clearly marked throughout the airport
- Airport layout is compact and generally easy for passengers to navigate
- DCA is the closest major airport to downtown Washington DC
- Commonly used by business, government, and corporate travelers
