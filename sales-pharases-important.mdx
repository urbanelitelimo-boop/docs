# **7. Polished Staff Lines & Phrases to Avoid**

---

## **7.1 Trust-Building Lines**

| Purpose | Suggested Phrase |
| :-- | :-- |
| Build confidence | "We handle this type of transportation regularly." |
| Build confidence | "We’d be happy to assist you with that." |
| Reassure simplicity | "We’ll make the process smooth and simple for you." |
| Emphasize professionalism | "We focus on reliable, professional service from start to finish." |
| Show attention to detail | "We want to make sure everything is handled properly for your trip." |

---

## **7.2 Closing Lines**

| Purpose | Suggested Phrase |
| :-- | :-- |
| Secure booking | "Would you like me to secure that for you now?" |
| Confirm booking | "I can finalize that for you today." |
| Confirm booking | "We can go ahead and confirm it if you're ready." |
| Confirm booking | "Shall I proceed with the reservation details?" |

---

## **7.3 Soft Urgency Lines**

| Purpose | Suggested Phrase |
| :-- | :-- |
| Availability reminder | "Availability can change depending on the date and vehicle." |
| Encourage timely action | "I’d recommend confirming sooner rather than later to secure the option." |
| Encourage timely action | "I’d be happy to lock this in for you while it’s available." |

---

## **7.4 Handling Hesitation**

| Purpose | Suggested Phrase |
| :-- | :-- |
| Empathize | "I completely understand." |
| Empathize | "That makes sense." |
| Give customer space | "Take your time, and I’ll be here to help." |
| Provide reassurance | "I can send everything in writing so you can review it comfortably." |

---

## **7.5 Phrases Team Should Avoid**

**Avoid Saying:**

- "I don’t know."
- "That’s not my department."
- "You have to wait."
- "That’s our price, take it or leave it."
- "I can’t do anything."
- "Maybe."
- "I think so."
- "You need to pay now or it’s gone."
- "This is the cheapest."
- "Our driver might be there."

**Better Replacements:**

| Weak Phrase | Professional Replacement |
| :-- | :-- |
| "I don’t know." | "Let me confirm that for you right away." |
| "That’s not my department." | "I’ll coordinate that for you with the appropriate team." |
| "I think so." | "Let me verify that and give you an accurate answer." |
