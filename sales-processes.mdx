# 1.1 Inbound Lead Handling

## Website Form Submissions

**Process Steps:**

| Step | Action |
| :-- | :-- |
| 1 | Lead Capture — Customer submits a quote request form on the website |
| 2 | Notification — Sales team receives instant email/CRM notification |
| 3 | Response Time Target — Contact customer within 15 min during business hours, within 1 hr outside business hours |
| 4 | Initial Review — Review form details: date, time, pickup/drop-off, vehicle preference, passenger count, occasion |
| 5 | Quote Preparation — Calculate pricing based on route, vehicle type, duration, and special requests |
| 6 | Customer Contact — Call or email the quote with full details |

**Phone Script – Responding to Web Form:**

> "Hi \[Customer Name\], this is \[Your Name\] from \[Company Name\]. Thank you for reaching out to us for your upcoming transportation on \[Date\]. I've reviewed your request and would love to help you find the perfect solution. Do you have a few minutes to discuss the details?"

**Email Template – Initial Quote:**

```text
Subject: Your Limousine Quote for [Date] – [Company Name]

Dear [Customer Name],

Thank you for considering [Company Name] for your upcoming transportation needs. Based on your request, I'm pleased to provide the following quote:

Trip Details:
- Date: [Date]
- Pickup Time: [Time]
- Pickup Location: [Address]
- Drop-off Location: [Address]
- Vehicle: [Vehicle Type]
- Passengers: [Number]
- Luggage: [Specify if any]
- Special Request: [If any]

Pricing:
- Base Rate: $[Amount]
- Gratuity (20%): $[Amount]
- Tolls/Parking (estimated): $[Amount]
- Administrative charges: [Amount]
- Total: $[Amount]

This quote is valid for 7 days. To confirm your booking, simply reply to this email or call me directly at [Phone Number].

I'm happy to answer any questions or adjust the details to better fit your needs.

Warm regards,
[Your Name]
[Title] | [Company Name]
[Phone] | [Email]
```

## Inbound Phone Calls

**Process Steps:**

| Step | Action |
| :-- | :-- |
| 1 | Answer within 3 rings — Professional greeting |
| 2 | Gather Information — Date, time, pickup/drop-off, vehicle preference, passenger count, occasion, special requests |
| 3 | Live Quote — Provide pricing during the call if possible |
| 4 | Trial Close — Ask if they'd like to proceed with booking |
| 5 | If No/Undecided — Capture email, explain follow-up process, send written quote |

**Phone Script – Inbound Inquiry:**

> "\[Company Name\], this is \[Your Name\], how may I assist you today?"\
> \
> After gathering details:\
> \
> "Based on what you've described—a \[vehicle type\] on \[date\] from \[pickup\] to \[drop-off\]—your total would be \$\[amount\], which includes gratuity and all fees including tolls. This includes \[key amenities\]. Would you like me to reserve that for you now?"\
> \
> If customer hesitates:\
> \
> "I completely understand you may want to think it over. Can I get your email address so I can send you all the details in writing? That way you'll have everything you need when you're ready to book."\
> \
> Or ask if I can text the quote to this number.

# 1.2 Outbound Sales Calls

**When to Use:**

- Following up on quotes not yet closed
- Re-engaging past customers for repeat business
- Calling leads from partner referrals\

**Phone Script – Follow-Up on Pending Quote:**

> "Hi \[Customer Name\], this is \[Your Name\] from \[Company Name\]. I'm following up on the quote I sent you for \[Date\]. I wanted to make sure you received everything and see if you had any questions I could answer?"

If customer mentions pricing concerns:

> "I appreciate you sharing that. Our pricing reflects the quality of our vehicles, professional chauffeurs, and reliability. That said, I'd like to make this work for you. Let me see what I can do—can I place you on a brief hold?"

# 1.3 Booking & Confirmation Process

## Step 1 – Collect Booking Information

| Field | Notes |
| :-- | :-- |
| Full name of passenger(s) | Required |
| Contact phone number | Required |
| Email address | Required |
| Complete pickup address | Include terminal/airline if airport pickup |
| Complete drop-off address | Full address |
| Number of passengers | Required |
| Number of luggage | Required |
| Flight number | Airport pickups |
| Special requests | Car seats, accessibility needs, beverages, signage |

## Step 2 – Payment Collection

**Option A – Card Over Phone:**

- Collect: Card number, expiration, CVV, billing zip
- Process through merchant terminal
- Confirm authorization
- Notify customer: "Your payment of \$\[amount\] has been processed successfully. You'll receive confirmation email and receipt."\

**Option B – Online Payment Link:**

- Generate secure payment link
- Send via email immediately
- Monitor completion within 24 hrs, follow up if not completed\

**Email Template – Payment Link:**

```text
Subject: Complete Your Booking – [Company Name]

Dear [Customer Name],

Thank you for choosing [Company Name]! To finalize your reservation for [Date], please complete the payment using the secure link below:

[CLICK HERE TO PAY SECURELY]

Booking Summary:
- Date: [Date]
- Pickup Time: [Time]
- Vehicle: [Vehicle Type]
- Total: $[Amount]

Once payment is received, you'll receive a detailed confirmation with your chauffeur information 24 hours before your trip.

Warm regards,
[Your Name]
```

# 1.4 Objection Handling

| Objection | Response |
| :-- | :-- |
| "It's too expensive" | "I understand budget is important. Our rate includes a professional chauffeur, vehicle insurance, gratuity, and 24/7 support. That said, let me see if we have any availability on a different vehicle that might work better for your budget." |
| "I need to check with my spouse/boss" | "Absolutely, that makes sense. What's the best time for me to follow up with you tomorrow? I'd hate for you to miss this date—we do have limited availability." |
| "I'm comparing prices" | "That's a smart approach. When comparing, I'd encourage you to ask about gratuity, fuel surcharges, and cancellation policy—we're fully transparent with no hidden fees. May I ask what you've seen so far?" |
| "I'll call you back" | "Of course! We do have several bookings for that date already. Would you like me to hold the vehicle for you for 24 hours while you decide? No obligation." |
| "Can you do better on price?" | "Let me see what I can do for you. \[Pause\] I can offer you \[discount/upgrade\]. This is our best rate for this vehicle. Would you like me to lock that in for you?" |
