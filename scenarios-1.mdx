## Timing-Related Scenarios

| Scenario | Standard Action | Important Notes |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Customer running late from pickup location | Inform customer about remaining airport time | Recalculate ETA immediately |
| Heavy traffic causing airport delay | Use alternate route if possible | Keep customer updated continuously |
| Customer requests earlier pickup | Adjust reservation timing if possible | Earlier pickup reduces airport stress |
| Customer cutting it too close to departure | Warn customer about risk of missing flight | Document timing recommendation provided |

---

## Terminal-Related Scenarios

| Scenario | Standard Action | Important Notes |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Wrong terminal confirmed during booking | Correct terminal before dispatch | Large airports may require extra transfer time |
| Customer changes terminal day of travel | Update chauffeur immediately | Recalculate route if needed |
| Airline changes terminal last minute | Verify updated terminal directly with airline | Common during operational disruptions |
| Customer unsure about airline terminal | Verify terminal before pickup begins | Never rely on customer assumptions |

---

## Curbside-Related Scenarios

| Scenario | Standard Action | Important Notes |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Excessive luggage causing slow unload | Assist efficiently and safely | May require additional unload time |
| Customer requests chauffeur to wait at curb | Explain airport curb waiting restrictions | Most airports prohibit extended stopping |
| Airport police instruct chauffeur to move | Follow airport enforcement instructions immediately | Safety and compliance come first |
| Customer leaves item at curb after departure | Coordinate safe return if possible | Airport loops may cause delays |

## Special Circumstance Scenarios

| Scenario | Standard Action | Important Notes |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Customer misses flight after drop-off | Follow company rebooking procedure if requested | New reservation may be required |
| Customer requests stop before airport | Confirm stop details before route begins | Additional charges may apply |
| Customer requests terminal escort | Confirm meet & assist availability | Parking fees may apply |
| Oversized luggage or equipment | Verify vehicle space before trip | Golf bags, skis, and instruments may require larger vehicles |
| Passenger traveling with pets | Confirm pet policy and carrier requirements | Notify chauffeur in advance |
| Corporate group using multiple terminals | Plan terminal sequence carefully | Timing coordination is critical |

---

## Weather & External Disruption Scenarios

| Scenario | Standard Action | Important Notes |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Severe weather causing delays | Allow additional travel time | Monitor traffic and airport conditions closely |
| Road closure near airport | Use alternate route immediately | Navigation adjustments may affect ETA |
| Accident on airport access road | Inform customer about delay | Recalculate route and timing |
| Airport departures suspended | Hold vehicle or return customer if requested | Follow customer instructions carefully |

# Shared Pickup & Drop-Off Scenarios

| Scenario | Standard Action | Important Notes |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Customer requests specific chauffeur | Check chauffeur availability before confirmation | VIP clients often request repeat chauffeurs |
| Last-minute booking changes | Update dispatch and chauffeur immediately | Reconfirm all trip details |
| Payment issue during service | Follow company billing procedure | Avoid conflict during active trip |
| Customer complaint during ride | Handle professionally and document issue | Escalate if necessary |
| Accident or incident during transport | Follow emergency and safety protocol | Dispatch management should be notified immediately |
| Customer leaves belongings in vehicle | Secure item and contact customer promptly | Document found property properly |
