## Flight-Related Scenarios

| Scenario | Standard Action | Important Notes |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Flight arrives on time | Follow normal pickup process | Monitor flight until passenger exits terminal |
| Flight delayed | Adjust chauffeur dispatch timing | Continue live flight tracking |
| Flight cancelled | Contact customer and confirm replacement flight | Update reservation and chauffeur schedule |
| Flight diverted to another airport | Confirm new airport and update dispatch | Additional charges may apply |
| Early arrival | Coordinate updated pickup timing | Chauffeur may still be en route |
| International arrival | Allow customs and immigration time | Meet & greet recommended for smoother coordination |
| Missed connecting flight | Rebook pickup using updated flight details | Confirm new terminal and ETA |

## Terminal & Location Scenarios

| Scenario | Standard Action | Important Notes |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Customer at wrong terminal | Guide customer to correct pickup area | Large airports may require transfer time |
| Airline changes terminal | Update chauffeur and customer immediately | Always monitor live flight updates |
| Customer exits wrong door/level | Guide customer using airport landmarks | Arrivals and departures are separate levels |
| Multiple passengers at different exits | Establish one official pickup point | Avoid multiple curb stops if possible |
| Customer has no cell signal | Follow standard wait-time policy | Common with international travelers |
| Confusion between curbside & meet-and-greet | Clarify pickup type immediately | Miscommunication causes major delays |

## Chauffeur-Related Scenarios

| Scenario | Standard Action | Important Notes |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Chauffeur running late | Notify customer immediately | Provide updated ETA |
| Chauffeur cannot locate customer | Confirm passenger location and terminal | Maintain active communication |
| Customer cannot locate chauffeur | Reconfirm vehicle details and pickup point | Use landmarks when guiding passenger |
| Vehicle breakdown | Dispatch replacement vehicle immediately | Keep customer updated continuously |
| Wrong vehicle dispatched | Correct assignment immediately | Verify vehicle before pickup |
| Chauffeur stuck in airport traffic | Notify passenger about delay | Common at large airports |

## Customer-Related Scenarios

| Scenario | Standard Action | Important Notes |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Excessive luggage | Confirm vehicle capacity | Upgrade vehicle if required |
| Passenger traveling with pets | Verify pet policy and vehicle suitability | Inform chauffeur beforehand |
| Wheelchair or special assistance needed | Arrange accessible assistance if available | Extra loading time may be required |
| Minor traveling alone | Follow company verification procedure | Ensure safe passenger transfer |
| VIP or celebrity traveler | Use privacy-focused coordination | Avoid unnecessary exposure |
| Customer does not speak English fluently | Use simple communication or translation support | Meet & greet may help significantly |
| First-time limo customer | Provide clear pickup instructions | Extra communication reduces confusion |

## Wait-Time Scenarios

| Scenario | Standard Action | Important Notes |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Customer exceeds free wait time | Apply company wait-time policy | Keep communication documented |
| Customer unreachable after landing | Attempt multiple contact methods | Follow no-show procedure if necessary |
| Customer requests extended wait | Confirm additional billing if applicable | Chauffeur availability may be affected |
| Passenger no-show | Follow official no-show procedure | Document all communication attempts |

## Group Pickup Scenarios

| Scenario | Standard Action | Important Notes |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Large group requires multiple vehicles | Coordinate all chauffeurs together | Confirm luggage volume in advance |
| Group arriving on different flights | Track each flight separately | Stagger chauffeur timing if needed |
| Group splitting to different destinations | Confirm routing before departure | May require multiple reservations |
