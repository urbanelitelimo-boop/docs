title: "SMS Scripts"
---

| Scenario | Text Message Template |
| :-- | :-- |
| **Quote Follow-Up** | "Hi \[Name\], this is \[Company Name\]. Just following up on the quote for your trip on \[Date\]. Let us know if you'd like to secure it." |
| **Payment Reminder** | "Hi \[Name\], this is \[Company Name\]. Your secure payment link has been sent by email. Once payment is completed, we’ll confirm your reservation." |
| **Booking Confirmation** | "Hi \[Name\], your reservation with \[Company Name\] is confirmed for \[Date/Time\]. We look forward to serving you." |
| **Driver Details** | "Hi \[Name\], your chauffeur for today’s ride is \[Driver Name\], driving a \[Vehicle\]. Contact: \[Phone\]. Thank you for choosing \[Company Name\]." |
| **Post-Service Feedback** | "Hi \[Name\], thank you for riding with \[Company Name\]. We hope you had a great experience and would love your feedback." |
