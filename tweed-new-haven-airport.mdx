Tweed New Haven Airport (HVN) is a small regional airport serving the New Haven, Connecticut area. The airport has a very compact single-terminal layout, making pickups quick, simple, and easy for both passengers and chauffeurs.

## Pickup Instructions

1. Passenger exits directly through the terminal arrivals area after landing
2. Passenger proceeds outside the terminal exit
3. Chauffeur meets passenger curbside directly outside the terminal
4. Pickup process is usually very quick with minimal walking distance

---

## Important Notes

- Single-terminal layout eliminates terminal confusion
- Chauffeurs are usually visible immediately upon passenger exit
- No AirTrain, shuttle, or internal transportation system is required
- Very short wait times are common for pickups
- One of the easiest airport pickup experiences in the region
