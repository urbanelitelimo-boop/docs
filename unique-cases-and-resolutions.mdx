---
title: "Pricing Questions"
---

| Customer Question | Suggested Staff Response |
| :-- | :-- |
| "How much will it cost?" | "I’d be happy to quote that for you. May I confirm the pickup date, time, locations, and passenger count so I can give you the most accurate rate?" |
| "Is that your final price?" | "That is the current rate for the details provided. If you'd like, I can also check whether there is another vehicle option that may better fit your budget." |
| "Do you charge by the hour or by the trip?" | "That depends on the type of service. Some reservations are quoted as point-to-point service, while others are based on hourly service. Once I have the trip details, I can advise the best option." |
