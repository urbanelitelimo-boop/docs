# Sedan

Sedans are ideal for airport transfers, corporate travel, city rides, and small-group transportation.

## Economy Sedan / Standard Sedan

Economy sedans are best suited for affordable and comfortable transportation for individuals or small groups.

### Common Vehicle Models

- Toyota Camry
- Honda Accord
- Toyota Corolla
- Honda Civic
- Hyundai Elantra
- Nissan Sentra

### Capacity

| Type | Capacity |
| :-- | :-- |
| Passengers | 3 |
| Luggage | 2 medium suitcases |

### Best Use Cases

- Airport transfers
- Daily city rides
- Budget-friendly transportation
- Solo travelers or couples

---

## Luxury Sedan / Executive Sedan

Luxury sedans provide enhanced comfort, premium interiors, and executive-level travel experiences.

### Common Vehicle Models

- Mercedes-Benz E-Class

- Lexus ES

### Capacity

| Type | Capacity |
| :-- | :-- |

- Executive airport pickups

# SUV

SUVs offer additional passenger seating and luggage space, making them ideal for families, groups, and premium transportation services.

## Full-Size SUV

Full-size SUVs provide spacious interiors and large luggage capacity for group transportation.

### Common Vehicle Models

- Chevrolet Suburban
- GMC Yukon
- Ford Expedition
- Cadillac Escalade

> Note: Cadillac Escalade and Lincoln Navigator can also be categorized under a separate **Premium SUV** tier.

### Capacity

| Type | Capacity |
| :-- | :-- |
| Passengers | Up to 6 comfortably |
| Luggage | Up to 7 suitcases |

### Best Use Cases

- Family transportation
- Group airport transfers
- Corporate groups
- Long-distance travel

---

## Mini Bus (Small Groups)

Mini buses are designed for smaller groups requiring efficient and comfortable transportation.

### Common Vehicle Models

- Ford E-Series Shuttle Bus
- Chevrolet Express Shuttle Bus

### Capacity

| Type | Capacity |
| :-- | :-- |
| Passengers | 14–24 passengers |

### Best Use Cases

- Small group events
- Hotel shuttles
- Corporate transportation
- Airport group transfers
