title: "Washington Dulles International Airport"
---

Washington Dulles International Airport (IAD) is a large international airport serving the Washington DC metropolitan area. The airport has a spread-out layout with multiple concourses connected to the main terminal through the underground AeroTrain system.

---

## Pickup Instructions

1. Passenger proceeds to baggage claim after landing
2. International passengers complete customs and immigration in the main terminal
3. Passenger exits to the arrivals/lower-level ground transportation area
4. Passenger proceeds to the designated car service pickup area
5. Chauffeur meets passenger curbside or inside terminal for meet & greet reservations

---

## Important Notes

- Main terminal handles both arrivals and ground transportation operations
- AeroTrain connects concourses internally to the main terminal
- International passengers exit through the main terminal after customs clearance
- Pickup occurs on the lower-level ground transportation area
- Allow additional travel and pickup time due to airport size and distance from downtown DC
- Traffic congestion is common during rush hours and busy travel periods
