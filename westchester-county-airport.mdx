Westchester County Airport (HPN) is a small regional airport serving the suburban New York market. The airport has a simple single-terminal layout with minimal congestion, making pickups significantly easier compared to larger New York airports like JFK and LGA.

## Pickup Instructions

1. Passenger proceeds to baggage claim after landing
2. Passenger exits directly to the main arrivals curb
3. Chauffeur meets passenger curbside or inside the arrivals hall for meet & greet reservations
4. Pickup process is usually quick with minimal walking distance

## Important Notes

- Single-terminal layout makes navigation very simple
- Minimal traffic and curbside congestion compared to JFK and LGA
- Chauffeurs can usually wait comfortably near the arrivals exit
- Popular airport for corporate travelers and executive transportation
- Pickup coordination is generally fast and straightforward
